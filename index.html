Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our barbershop services.

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

We accept both appointments and walk-ins. However, appointments are recommended, especially during busy hours and weekends, to minimize your wait time. You can book an appointment through our website or by calling us directly.

How long does a typical haircut take?

A standard haircut typically takes about 30-45 minutes. More complex styles, color treatments, or additional services like beard trims may take longer. We pride ourselves on taking the necessary time to ensure quality results rather than rushing through appointments.

What form of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay. We currently do not accept personal checks.

Do you offer any loyalty programs or discounts?

Yes, we offer a loyalty program where you earn points for each visit. After collecting a certain number of points, you can redeem them for free services or products. We also offer discounts for students, military personnel, and seniors. Ask our staff for more details during your visit.

What services do you offer besides haircuts?

Besides haircuts, we offer a range of services including beard trims and styling, hot towel shaves, hair coloring, highlights, facial treatments, scalp massages, and hair treatments for thinning hair. We also sell professional hair care products that our barbers recommend.
